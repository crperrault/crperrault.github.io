The Thomas and Gilpin Families of Coalbrookdale

Mary Smith, who married William Benington, was Arthur Benington’s grandmother. She descends from Mark Gilpin and Mary Thomas of Coalbrookdale, Shropshire, where their families were involved in the 18th century in some of the most important innovations in the history of iron making and contributed to launching the industrial revolution.

Les familles Thomas et Gilpin de Coalbrookdale

Mary Smith, qui épousa William Benington, était la grand-mère d’Arthur Benington. Elle descend de Mark Gilpin et Mary Thomas, originaires de Coalbrookdale, dans le Shropshire, où leurs familles ont participé, au XVIIIe siècle, à certaines des innovations les plus importantes de l’histoire de la fabrication du fer et ont contribué au lancement de la révolution industrielle.
