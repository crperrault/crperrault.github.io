The Family of Jean-Emmanuel Dumoulin

Jean-Emmanuel Dumoulin, a Protestant Swiss, settled in Montreal shortly after the Conquest. There he married Charlotte Duchouquet and worked as a merchant for about fifteen years before moving to Cahokia, in the Illinois Territory, where he became well known as a land speculator and judge. He died there intestate in 1805, leaving an estate that would not be settled until 1823.
